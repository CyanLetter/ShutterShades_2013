Marc McKee

Model: Stick-O-Rama
Manufacturer: World Industries
Year: 1990

The Stick-O-Rama deck was a radical concept conceived by artist Marc McKee for World Industries in 1990. The deck itself was screened with an empty street scene that the user could customize with 19 stickers included with the board.

The sticker collection that came with the board was an interesting one and according to an advertisement for the board included “pimps, whores, vagrants, men in blue and all your other friends from playland.”

The board was wildly popular as skaters went crazy for the opportunity to create their own graphic by placing the sticker characters on the scene of an inner-city neighborhood.
